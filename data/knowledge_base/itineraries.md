# 7-Day Classic Vietnam Itinerary

Best for first-time visitors who want the highlights of north + central.

**Day 1 — Arrive Hanoi**: airport transfer to Old Quarter hotel. Evening walk to Hoan Kiem Lake, dinner Bun Cha or Pho.

**Day 2 — Hanoi city**: morning Temple of Literature + Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum complex (closes 11:00). Afternoon Old Quarter walking + egg coffee at Cafe Giang. Evening Water Puppet show (Thang Long Theatre).

**Day 3 — Halong Bay 2D1N cruise**: pickup 08:00 from hotel, ~3h drive. Lunch onboard, kayaking, sunset on deck.

**Day 4 — Halong cruise day 2**: morning cave + Ti Top island swim, lunch onboard, return Hanoi by 17:00. Evening overnight train to Hue (option) or fly to Da Nang next morning.

**Day 5 — Fly to Da Nang / drive to Hoi An**: 45min flight Hanoi → DAD, 45min taxi DAD → Hoi An (400k VND). Afternoon Ancient Town walking + lantern release on Thu Bon river.

**Day 6 — Hoi An full day**: morning Tra Que vegetable village + cooking class. Afternoon free for tailor fitting / beach. Evening night market.

**Day 7 — Hoi An → My Son → depart**: morning My Son Cham ruins half-day tour. Afternoon DAD airport transfer + flight home.

**Indicative budget** (per person, 2026, mid-range double occupancy):
- Hotels (6 nights): 4.5M VND.
- Halong cruise 2D1N (4★): 5M VND.
- Domestic flight HAN → DAD: 1M VND.
- Tours + entrance: 2.5M VND.
- Food + transfer: 2.5M VND.
- **Total per person: ~15.5M VND (≈ USD 620)**, excluding international flight.

# 10-Day North + South Itinerary

Adds Sapa + HCMC + Mekong to the classic route.

**Day 1**: Arrive Hanoi.
**Day 2**: Hanoi city.
**Day 3**: Overnight train to Sapa (departs ~22:00).
**Day 4**: Arrive Sapa, half-day Lao Chai → Ta Van trek. Homestay night.
**Day 5**: Cat Cat or Fansipan cable car. Evening sleeper bus or train back to Hanoi (or fly next morning from Lao Cai's nearest airport at Dien Bien).
**Day 6**: Arrive Hanoi, fly to Da Nang (afternoon), transfer Hoi An.
**Day 7**: Hoi An full day.
**Day 8**: Fly HAN → SGN (1.5h). Afternoon Cu Chi Tunnels tour. Evening rooftop drink at Bitexco / Landmark 81.
**Day 9**: Mekong Delta day trip (Ben Tre or My Tho).
**Day 10**: HCMC War Remnants Museum + Reunification Palace, depart.

**Indicative budget**: ~22M VND per person (≈ USD 880) excluding international flight.

# 14-Day Grand Vietnam Itinerary

For travellers wanting depth + beach time. Pace: 1 region every 4–5 days.

**Days 1–4 — North**: Hanoi (2 days) + Halong cruise (2 days, 1 night). Optional: add Sapa 2 days (-2 from southern leg).
**Days 5–8 — Central**: train or fly Hanoi → Hue. Hue 2 days (imperial citadel, royal tombs). Drive Hai Van Pass → Da Nang/Hoi An, 2 days (Ancient Town, beach, cooking class).
**Days 9–11 — South**: fly Da Nang → HCMC. HCMC 1.5 days + Mekong Delta 1.5 days (overnight Ben Tre or Can Tho).
**Days 12–14 — Beach finale**: fly HCMC → Phu Quoc. 3 days beach + Hon Thom cable car + sunset.

**Indicative budget**: ~32M VND per person (≈ USD 1,280) for mid-range, ~55M VND (USD 2,200) for 4★+ private guide.
