# General Safety in Vietnam

Vietnam is generally safe for foreign visitors. Violent crime against tourists is rare. The main risks are **opportunistic petty theft**, **traffic**, and **food/water-borne illness**.

**Petty theft hotspots**:
- HCMC District 1 (Bui Vien, Dong Khoi) — drive-by phone snatching from motorbike. Hold your phone with two hands and away from the road edge.
- Hanoi Old Quarter — pickpocketing during the weekend walking street.
- Beach resorts (Mui Ne, Nha Trang) — leave nothing on the beach when swimming.

**Traffic**: Vietnam has one of the highest road-fatality rates in Southeast Asia. As a pedestrian: walk slowly and steadily across the road; let motorbikes weave around you. Do NOT stop or run.

**Scams to know**:
- "Free" cyclo / xich lo ride that demands USD 50 at the end. Agree price in writing first.
- "Tour" / "tailor" touts at airport claiming your hotel is closed. Walk past.
- Taxi meter running 3x normal speed in airport pickups — use Grab or the airport bus.
- Fake monks blessing bracelets and demanding payment. Politely decline.

# Health & Vaccinations for Vietnam

**Routine vaccinations**: ensure MMR, Tdap, polio, influenza are up to date.

**Recommended for most travellers**:
- **Hepatitis A** — food/water-borne, recommended for all visitors.
- **Typhoid** — especially if eating street food extensively.
- **Tetanus booster** if last dose >10 years ago.

**Recommended for longer stays / rural travel**:
- **Hepatitis B** — long-term travellers, medical exposure risk.
- **Japanese Encephalitis** — long rural stays in rice-growing areas during monsoon.
- **Rabies pre-exposure** — long stays, frequent contact with animals.

**Malaria**: low risk in most tourist areas (HCMC, Hanoi, Halong, Hoi An, Da Nang are malaria-free). Some risk in rural Central Highlands and Mekong Delta provinces. Consult CDC / WHO maps for current guidance.

**Dengue**: present year-round, peak May–November. Use DEET-based repellent (30%+). No vaccine for tourists.

**Travel insurance**: strongly recommended. Confirm coverage includes:
- Hospital evacuation (USD 200,000+).
- Motorbike accidents (often excluded unless you hold a valid IDP licence).
- COVID-related cancellations.

**Hospitals**:
- Hanoi: Vinmec Times City, Hanoi French Hospital.
- HCMC: FV Hospital (Phu My Hung), Vinmec Central Park.
- Da Nang: Family Medical Practice.
